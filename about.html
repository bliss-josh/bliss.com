Our History

The journey of Namirembe Parents' Primary and Nursery School since its founding.

Namirembe Parents' Primary and Nursery School was officially opened on 5th January, 2003 by the then Mayor of Kampala John Ssebaana Kizito with 230 pupils only. This number has since gradually grown to over 1000 pupils and 120 staff. The proprietors of the school are Mr. Denis Kazibwe and Mrs. Sarah Nabachwa Kazibwe who have a great passion for education hence the genesis of founding our great institution. The school has a functioning school management committee composed of highly profiled individuals responsible for planning, monitoring and directing all school programs.
